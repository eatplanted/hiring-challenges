#Role model​
 
- We stand for our Values and promote our culture. ​

- We are coaches and first point of contact for our Circle members.​

- We provide orientation, shape the Circle vision and win over our Circle members.​

​

#Accountability​

- We develop solutions and make decisions.​

- We enable development and transfer competence.​

- We take responsibility for the Circle’s needs and mistakes.​

​

Communication​

- We inform proactively, communicate clearly and in line with the target group.​

- We give feedback and ask for it.​

- We ensure the transfer of knowledge inside, into and out of the Circle.​
